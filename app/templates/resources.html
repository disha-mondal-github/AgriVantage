Educational Resources

Explore the following resources to enhance your understanding of Earth observation data and its applications in agriculture:

  • Agriculture and Water Management Pathfinder: Drought, vegetation health, and soil moisture can be tracked remotely.
  • Floods Data Pathfinder: NASA provides datasets and tools for flood response and mitigation.
  • Extreme Heat Data Pathfinder: NASA data aids in forecasting and monitoring extreme heat events.
  • NASA Acres: Focuses on applying satellite EO information to agricultural and food security challenges.
  • NASA Harvest: A consortium working on environmental, economic, and social aspects of agriculture.
  • AppEEARS: The Application for Extracting and Exploring Analysis Ready Samples
